Air Fryer Blackened Chicken Breasts

Cook these blackened chicken breasts in your air fryer. Now there is no need for a dirty cast iron skillet and oily chicken!



Ingredients

  • 2 teaspoons paprika
  • 1 teaspoon ground thyme
  • 1 teaspoon cumin
  • 1/2 teaspoon cayenne pepper
  • 1/2 teaspoon onion powder
  • 1/2 teaspoon black pepper
  • 1/4 teaspoon salt
  • 2 teaspoons vegetable oil
  • 2 chicken breast halves skinless

Directions

Step 1

Mix paprika, thyme, cumin, cayenne, onion powder, black pepper, and salt together in a bowl; transfer to a large plate.

Step 2

Preheat the air fryer to 360 degrees F.

Step 3

Rub oil over each chicken breast until fully coated, then press into spice mixture until all sides are coated. Let sit for 5 minutes while the air fryer preheats. Place into the air fryer basket.

Step 4

Air-fry chicken until no longer pink in the center and the juces run clear, about 20 minutes, flipping halfway through. An instant-read thermometer inserted into the center should read at least 165 degrees F.

Step 5

Transfer chicken on to a plate and wait to cool.
